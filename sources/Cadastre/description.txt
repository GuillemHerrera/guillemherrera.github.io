<h1>
    Cadastro Analisys
</h1>
<p>
Analyzing data of Spanish register of edifications and the Urbanistic Planning, we can visualize how differs the construccions real height  over plannificated ones. 
</p>
<p>
   Grey area for regulated construccions, Green area for height that is still allow to build, Red for construccions outside regulation.
</p>
